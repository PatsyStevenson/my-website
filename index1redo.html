About Me

Advocating for Gender Equality

I am an award-winning campaigner, writer, and public speaker advocating for women and girls in the public eye since 2021. My TEDx Talk in 2023 focused on the importance of gender balance in AI creation.

Winner of Harper's Bazaar Woman of the Year award for Campaigning in 2023 and named one of The Observer's 'Faces of 2021'. I serve as an ambassador for Selfhood, a charity advocating for gender liberation, and for People's Trust For Endangered Species.

Currently writing my debut book on gender equality and AI, I consult on gender equality for professionals ranging from film directors to business owners.
